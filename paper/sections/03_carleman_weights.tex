\section{Carleman weights}

Carleman estimates introduce exponential weights that transform pointwise or localized smallness into global weighted estimates.
A typical weighted object has the form
\[
v(x,t) = e^{\phi(x,t)} u(x,t).
\]

In the pipeline, this step changes the scale of the problem while preserving the equation structure.
Same math, new scale: hidden math must extend step-by-step and resist collapse under the constraints.
