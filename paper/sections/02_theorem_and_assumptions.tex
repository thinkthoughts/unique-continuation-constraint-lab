\section{Theorem and assumptions}

We consider
\[
i\partial_t u = -\Delta u + V(x)u.
\]

\begin{theorem}
If a solution exhibits sufficiently strong Gaussian decay at two distinct times,
then \(u \equiv 0\).
\end{theorem}
