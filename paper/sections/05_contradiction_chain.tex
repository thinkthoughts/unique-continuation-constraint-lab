\section{Contradiction chain}

The proof combines:

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\arabic*)]
\item two-time decay assumptions,
\item Carleman-weighted estimates,
\item Hardy-type variation constraints,
\item the Schrödinger equation structure.
\end{enumerate}

Non-zero solutions may satisfy individual constraints, but not all simultaneously.

Thus, assuming a non-zero solution leads to incompatible requirements.

\medskip

\noindent
\textbf{Conclusion.}
Only the zero solution satisfies all constraints.
