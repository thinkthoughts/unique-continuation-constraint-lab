\begin{abstract}
This note presents a constraint-based bridge for unique continuation arguments in Schr\"odinger equations.
The standard proof structure is preserved: two-time decay, Carleman weights, Hardy-type inequalities, contradiction, and the zero solution.
The contribution is expository: a reproducible pipeline for making the proof structure easier to inspect.
\end{abstract}
