\begin{abstract}
We present an expository, reproducible view of unique continuation for Schrödinger equations.
The standard proof structure—two-time decay, Carleman weighting, Hardy-type variation constraints, and contradiction—is preserved.
Our contribution is organizational: a pipeline of figures and companion notebooks that align explanation, computation, and visualization.
\end{abstract}
